Accommodation

Passau is a touristy town all year round. We were able to secure a limited number of reserved rooms available for students of the fall school – see below. Please book yourself. Of course, you are free to choose a different place for your stay.

  • Rotel Inn: single rooms/shared bathroom for 45 €/night/person (Sept. 15-27 2024), reserved until August 15. Please send email with 'Fall School' to info@rotel-inn.de.

There are two more budget options we recommend, though we have not reserved rooms there:

  • Art hotel (in town, shared rooms, but not barrier-free!)
  • Spectrum Kirche offers single and double rooms (20-min walk or bus connection to the venue, up the hill, lovely garden and view)
