Program



We offer four (on-site) courses, each of which consists of ten 90-minute lectures. All courses are taught in English.

11am-12.30pm		 Python for Language Processing (Jakob Prange, University of Augsburg)
1.30-3pm, 3.30-5pm Multimodal CL and NLP: Combining Language and Vision for (Computational) Semantics (Carina Silberer, IMS Stuttgart)
Week II (23-27 September 2024) 9-10.30am
11am-12.30pmtime*		 Argument Mining (John Lawrence, University of Dundee)
1.30-3pm, 3.30-5pm Visual Analytics for Linguistics (AI group ETH Zurich)

Students can receive credits for the courses under the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS).
