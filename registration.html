Registration

Early-bird registration will open in April 2024 and will run until 1 July 2024. Regular registration closes on 31 August 2024.

  • Early bird fee: 100 EUR (non-students: 150 EUR)
  • Regular fee: 150 EUR (non-students: 200 EUR)

The registration fee includes:

  • Admission to all courses and events of the fall school
  • Coffee breaks throughout the fall school

Support grants

DGfS-CL and GSCL are jointly offering stipends for students who want to take part in the CL Fall School 2024. The stipend covers the registration costs and, depending on the number of applications, part of the travel and accommodation expenses. Deadline for application is June 30, 2024 (AoE).


Eligibility criteria:

  • Applicants must be registered students at an academic institution.
  • Applicants must be members of either DGfS-CL or GSCL (membership applications can be submitted in parallel to the stipend application, see here for the DGfS-CL application and here for the GSCL membership application).

Application documents:

  • Name of application, home institution, study subject
  • Copy of your national ID
  • Proof of matriculation
  • CV
  • 300 words on your motivation to take part in the fall school

Submit the application as one single PDF to clfallschool2024@fim.uni-passau.de.


If there is a need for partial reimbursement of travel and accommodation costs, we will ask for justification and proof of the costs incurred. The decision on the corresponding reimbursement is made by the DGfS-CL and GSCL boards; the application is of course treated confidentially. However, the refund will not be made until December 2024, after all student funding applications have been taken into account.


The scholarship can be mentioned in the CV as “DGfS-CL & GSCL Fall School Scholarship”.
